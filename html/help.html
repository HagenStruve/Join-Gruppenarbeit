What is Join?

Join is a popular Lean workflow management method for defining, managing, and improving services that deliver knowledge work. It helps you visualize work, maximize efficiency, and improve continuously. Work is represented on Join boards, allowing you to optimize work delivery across multiple teams and handle even the most complex projects in a single environment.

How to use it

Summary

Here you can find a quick overview about the actual tasks. The Summary shows you how many tasks are on the board, how many tasks are in work and witch of them are urgent to take care of.

Board

All created tasks will be showed in the board. Per drag and drop you can move the tasks to the different categories. The categories are "To do", "In progress", "Awaiting Feedback" and "Done". When you click on a task you can edit the task and also get a detail overview with all Information. You can also search for tasks and add new tasks.

Add Task

On Add Task you can create a new Task. It's important that all fields are filled and that you add a category and the responsible person. Also don't forget to say how urgent the task have to be done. When you click on the Button "Create Task", the task will be showed on the board.

Contacts

Here you can find all your contacts and create new contacts. When you click on a existing contact you can see the E-Mail Adress and the Phone Number. You can also edit the contact.
